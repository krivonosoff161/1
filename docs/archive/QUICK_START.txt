=====================================
  OKX TRADING BOT - QUICK START
=====================================

FILES:
------
start_bot.bat  - Start the bot
stop_bot.bat   - Stop the bot
view_logs.bat  - View logs (interactive)


HOW TO USE:
-----------

1. START BOT:
   - Double click: start_bot.bat
   - Wait for "Starting bot in DEMO mode..."
   - Bot is running!

2. VIEW LOGS:
   - Double click: view_logs.bat
   - Choose option:
     1 = Last 50 lines
     2 = Last 100 lines
     4 = Real-time monitoring (RECOMMENDED!)
     7 = Show only trading signals

3. STOP BOT:
   - Double click: stop_bot.bat
   OR
   - Press Ctrl+C in bot window


CONFIGURATION:
--------------
Trading pairs: BTC-USDT, ETH-USDT, SOL-USDT
Mode: DEMO (safe testing)
Risk per trade: 1%
Max positions: 3


WHAT TO EXPECT:
---------------
- 3-8 signals per hour (if market is active)
- 1-3 positions open at the same time
- SOL will give more signals (high volatility)


MONITORING:
-----------
GOOD SIGNS:
- "SIGNAL GENERATED: SOL-USDT BUY"
- "POSITION OPENED"
- "Partial TP executed"
- "Break-even activated"

BAD SIGNS:
- Many "Stop Loss hit" in a row
- "MAX CONSECUTIVE LOSSES REACHED"
- Win Rate < 40%


SAFETY:
-------
- Maximum 3 positions at once
- Each position = 5% of balance
- Risk per trade = 1% of balance
- Daily loss limit = 10%
- Auto stop after 3 losses in a row


TROUBLESHOOTING:
----------------
Problem: Bot doesn't start
Solution: Check that venv folder exists

Problem: No signals
Solution: Normal! Bot waits for good conditions

Problem: Can't stop bot
Solution: Run stop_bot.bat twice


SUPPORT:
--------
Check logs with: view_logs.bat -> option 6 (errors)

Step 2: Double click view_logs.bat -> choose 4
Step 3: Watch trading in real-time!
